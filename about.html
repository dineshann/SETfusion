Who we are

A turnkey developer, not just a supplier.

Possessing cogent and comprehensive experience in the power solutions industry, SETfusion serves as a turnkey developer of MW-level PV power plants. Working with a mission to achieve grid parity and reduce cost, we offer customized power plant solutions — spanning innovative solar solutions to turnkey EPC for solar power plants, and customized solutions for roof-top and off-grid installations.

As a supplier of solar installations and ecological energy technology, we deliver integrated sustainable energy production solutions. That's what our team is committed to. Our product portfolio spans solar systems for private and commercial users as well as the public sector.

One-stop solar energy supplier

Everything Under One Roof

We offer complete solar pump controllers, solar inverters, solar PCUs, solar water systems, on-grid and off-grid inverters, solar power storage systems, mounting systems and know-how. In the solar products segment, we are a leading manufacturer.
