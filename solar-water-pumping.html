Overview

Built for farmers who can't wait on the grid.

Agricultural power supply is rarely on the farmer's schedule. SETfusion's solar pumping systems remove that dependency altogether — pulling water for irrigation right through the hours of daylight when it's needed most, with no diesel, no waiting for load-shedding to end, and no monthly electricity bill for the pump.

Every installation is built around SETfusion's own SWP-series controllers, sized from 0.5 HP up to 100 HP, paired with solar PV modules and a matching mounting structure. The controller runs an onboard MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) algorithm that continuously adjusts the pump's operating voltage and current, drawing measurably more usable power out of the same panel array — typically a 35–40% efficiency gain over a non-MPPT setup.

Both surface and submersible configurations are available, and systems are engineered to work with pumps you may already own — so an existing installation can often be converted to solar rather than replaced outright.
