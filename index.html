About SETfusion

Built by engineers who install what they design.

Since 2015, SETfusion has manufactured solar power electronics from our own facility in Bengaluru — not sourced, assembled and re‑labelled. That's how we back every controller, inverter and PCU with real after‑sales support, and how we keep pricing honest without cutting engineering corners. Our systems now run across 1000+ residential, agricultural, industrial and government sites, and we're MNRE empanelled and BIS / ISO certified for tender‑ready procurement.

Before you ask

Common Questions

Most residential rooftop systems up to 3kW qualify for the central Surya Ghar subsidy, and we handle the MNRE documentation as part of the project. Eligibility depends on your sanctioned load and DISCOM — we'll confirm the exact amount during your free site survey.

Residential and farm systems are typically installed and commissioned within 1–3 weeks of the site survey, once the components are manufactured. Larger commercial and government tender projects are scoped with a project timeline upfront.

Because we manufacture the electronics ourselves, we stand behind them directly rather than routing you to a third-party brand. Warranty terms vary by product — we'll spell out exact coverage and service response times in your quote.

Yes — we're BIS certified, ISO certified and MNRE empanelled, and we prepare tender-ready documentation as part of the project for government, housing society and institutional procurement.

We're based in Yeshwanthpur / Jalahalli, Bengaluru, and actively serve residential, agricultural, industrial and government sites across Bengaluru and the surrounding region. Message us your location on WhatsApp and we'll confirm coverage.
